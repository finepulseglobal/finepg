FinePulse operates across all four primary freight modes: road (FTL, LTL, and express), air (scheduled and charter), sea (FCL, LCL, and breakbulk), and warehousing with fulfilment. Each mode is managed by dedicated specialists and integrated into a single tracking platform so you always have a consolidated view of your cargo.

Submit your shipment details through our contact form — origin, destination, cargo type, weight, and preferred transit time. A dedicated account manager will respond within 2 business hours with a tailored quote. For recurring volume or contract freight, we offer dedicated pricing structures reviewed quarterly.

Yes. FinePulse is certified to handle ADR (road), IATA DGR (air), and IMDG (sea) classified cargo. For temperature-sensitive freight, we operate controlled-temperature vehicles and cold-chain compliant warehousing. All handlers are trained and recertified annually.

FinePulse has in-house customs brokerage in 18 countries, with partner agents covering an additional 34 markets. We prepare all documentation — commercial invoices, certificates of origin, packing lists, and HS classification — and handle duty deferment where applicable. You receive a pre-clearance status update before arrival at the border.

Transit times vary by origin, destination, cargo type, customs requirements, and service level. Domestic and regional road freight is quoted lane by lane, air freight can move urgently when airline capacity is available, and ocean freight typically ranges from several weeks depending on port pair and sailing schedule. Every quote includes a realistic estimated transit window.

There is no hard minimum. We work with businesses of all sizes, from single-pallet LTL consignments to multi-trailer FTL programmes.
