Assist the customer care team with client communication, inquiry tracking, quote follow-ups, and shipment update coordination so customers feel informed before, during, and after cargo movement.

Key Responsibilities

Hiring Process

What to Expect

Honest answers to the questions candidates ask us most before and after applying.

Our intern and NYSC process has three stages: an initial 30-minute call, a practical task relevant to the role, and a final conversation with the hiring team. For most openings, the full process takes one to two weeks from application to offer. We do not use automated screening systems; every application is read by a human.

Current intern and NYSC roles are anchored in Lagos so candidates can learn directly with the team. Some work may be flexible by function, but onboarding, reviews, and practical training happen with regular in-person support.
